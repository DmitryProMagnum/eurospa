Privacy Policy

Last updated · June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EuroSpa ("we", "us", "our"), a licensed massage and spa centre located at the Ramada by Wyndham Hotel, 2nd floor, Barsha Heights, Dubai, United Arab Emirates, handles information collected through this website.

Information we collect

This website does not host a customer account system and does not process payments online. We may receive the following information only when you choose to contact us:

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name and phone number entered in our booking form or quiz, which are sent to us via WhatsApp when you submit them.
  • Usage data, anonymised analytics such as pages viewed, device type, approximate region and button clicks, collected via Google Analytics 4 to help us improve the site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to booking requests and enquiries you send us.
  • To arrange, confirm and manage your appointment.
  • To understand how visitors use our website and improve our services.

WhatsApp

Our booking flow opens WhatsApp with a pre filled message. When you send that message you communicate with us directly through WhatsApp, whose own privacy terms apply. We use your number solely to arrange your appointment and do not share it for marketing purposes without consent.

Analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics 4, which sets cookies and collects anonymised usage data. For details on the cookies used and how to control them, see our Cookie Policy.

Third party content

Pages may embed third party content such as Google Maps and an Instagram feed (Elfsight). These providers may collect data in line with their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We keep booking related messages only as long as needed to provide our services and to meet legal or accounting obligations, after which they are deleted.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information you have shared with us. To make a request, contact us using the details below.
