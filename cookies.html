Cookie Policy

Last updated · June 2026

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. This page explains which cookies the EuroSpa website uses and why. This website does not use cookies to sell products or run advertising on our own behalf.

Cookies we use

ProviderPurposeType
Google Analytics 4Anonymised statistics on how visitors use the site, such as pages, device, region and button clicksAnalytics
Google MapsDisplays our location map and may set cookies when the map loadsFunctional, third party
Elfsight Instagram feedDisplays our Instagram feed and may set cookies when the widget loadsFunctional, third party

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and set most browsers to block them. Blocking cookies may affect how embedded maps or the Instagram feed display, but the core of the website will continue to work.

Opting out of analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics across all websites, you can install the Google Analytics Opt out Browser Add on.
